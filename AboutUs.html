About Us




Welcome to Glass Ceiling! Ella, Callie, Lydia, and Kristen developed this website for women in engineering, just like ourselves. We are all sophomores at Iowa State majoring in Software Enginerring. It's hard to know whether companies with "inclusive environments" actually provide a safe place for their female employees to grow and be heard. We want women from all over to share their experiences, because we are all in this together.
